## 技能
- **Mindlock**：While a Tech-Priest model is leading this model’s unit, improve the Ballistic Skill and Weapon Skill characteristics of this model’s weapons by 1.
- **Bound Creation**：While a Tech-Priest model is leading this model’s unit, that <span class="kwb">TECH-PRIEST</span> model has the Feel No Pain 5+ ability.
- **SERVITOR BODYGUARD**：At the start of the Declare Battle Formations step, this model can join one other unit from your army that is being led by a Tech-Priest Infantry model. If it does, until the end of the battle, this model counts as being part of that Bodyguard unit, and that Bodyguard unit’s Starting Strength is increased accordingly.
